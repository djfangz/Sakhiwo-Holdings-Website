    About Us

    Sakhiwo Holdings is a financial credit services provider as set h the South Africa National Credit Regulator. Sakhiwo Holdings renders world class Financial Empowerment services, Micro-credit and Finance Skills Acquisition training to the community of KwaZulu Natal previously underserved by the Credit sector of the economy.

    Our Mission

    We aspire to be the leading provider of cutting edge Finance and Credit Empowerment Solutions that promotes prosperity and a value added culture not only to Peit Retief and Danhuiser but to the whole of KwaZulu Natal.

    Our Plan

    We are an accountability and trust based business and our commitment to integrity and honesty to our customers and everyone we work with is recommended by the National Credit Regulator in South Africa.

    Our Vision

    To pioneer homegrown cutting edge Credit and Finance Empowerment Solutions that will deliver relevant Credit and Financial knowledge and assistance to communities that are often overlooked and underserved by other financial institutions.
