    About Us

    Sakhiwo Finance is registered with the South African National Credit Regulator Registration No. NCRCP16759 NCR as a Credit and Finance services provider. Sakhiwo Finance specialises in not only Credit and Finance Empowerment services but other services such as Micro-Credit, Micro-Loans and Finance Skills Acquisition training to previously under served communities in Vryheid, Dannhauser and KwaZulu Natal.

    Our Mission

    At Sakhiwo Finance we aspire to be the home of choice for world class Finance and Credit Services that drive empowerment, promote prosperity and bring wealth to Vryheid, Dannhauser and the whole of KwaZulu Natal.

    Our Plan

    Accountability and Integrity is the foundation on which Sakhiwo built guided by compliance to the National Credit Regulator NCR, Financial Services Board FSB and FICA FICA,.

    Our Vision

    To pioneer homegrown cutting edge Credit and Finance Empowerment Solutions that will deliver relevant Credit and Financial services to communities that are often overlooked and underserved.
